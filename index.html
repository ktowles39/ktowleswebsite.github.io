Early Covid Data in SQL

In this project, I conducted a comprehensive analysis of early COVID-19 data using a combination of pgAdmin, Microsoft SQL, and Tableau for dynamic visualization. The primary data source was the extensive repository available at https://ourworldindata.org/. Noteworthy insights emerged from this analysis, including the rapid pace of vaccination rollout, identification of countries with the highest infection rates, and a deep examination of COVID-19's impact on both individual countries and entire continents in terms of mortality rates.

Airbnb Data in SQL

In this project, we harnessed the power of Microsoft SQL, pgAdmin, Excel, and Tableau to conduct an in-depth analysis of publicly available Airbnb property data within the vibrant city of Washington, D.C. Our exploration unearthed fascinating insights, including the average price per bedroom, the neighborhood-specific average rental prices, and annual revenue figures for hosts.
